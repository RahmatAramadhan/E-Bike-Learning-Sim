Battery

The battery is a crucial component of an e-bike, providing the necessary power to the motor. It is typically rechargeable and can vary in capacity, affecting the range and performance of the e-bike.

Battery stringing refers to the configuration of multiple battery cells connected in series or parallel to achieve the desired voltage and capacity. Proper stringing is essential for optimal performance and longevity of the battery system.
